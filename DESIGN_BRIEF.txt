========================================
DESIGN BRIEF FOR HOTEL CONCIERGE DASHBOARD
========================================

🎯 PROJECT: Athens Concierge - Hotel Portal Dashboard

📋 PLATFORM: Web Application (React)

---

## 1. BUSINESS CONTEXT

**What it is:**
A commission-based booking platform where hotel concierges book restaurants for their guests.

**How it works:**
- Hotel concierges browse restaurants and make bookings
- Restaurants confirm/complete bookings
- Hotels earn €3 commission per completed booking
- Platform tracks all bookings and earnings

**Users:**
Hotel concierge staff (primary users for this dashboard)

---

## 2. DASHBOARD REQUIREMENTS

### NAVIGATION
- Sidebar OR top navigation (designer's choice)
- 3 main sections:
  1. My Bookings (default view)
  2. Browse Restaurants
  3. Earnings
- Logout button
- Quick stats visible (total bookings count, monthly earnings)

### VIEW 1: MY BOOKINGS (Most Important)
**Purpose:** Manage all restaurant reservations

**Content to display:**
- List/table of all bookings showing:
  - Restaurant name
  - Guest name
  - Date & time
  - Number of guests
  - Guest phone number
  - Status (pending/confirmed/completed)
  - Commission earned (€3 if completed, €0 if not)

**Features:**
- Filter buttons: All, Pending, Confirmed, Completed
- Status badges with colors:
  - Pending = Yellow
  - Confirmed = Blue
  - Completed = Green

**Sample data:**
- Taverna Plaka | Smith Family | 2024-06-15 19:30 | 4 guests | Pending
- Plaka Grill | John Doe | 2024-06-20 20:00 | 2 guests | Confirmed
- Italian Corner | Anderson Group | 2024-06-18 19:00 | 6 guests | Completed

### VIEW 2: BROWSE RESTAURANTS
**Purpose:** Find and book restaurants for guests

**Content to display:**
- List/cards of restaurants showing:
  - Restaurant name
  - Cuisine type (Greek, Italian, Seafood, etc.)
  - Location (Plaka, Kolonaki, etc.)
  - Price range (€€, €€€, €€€€)
  - Rating (4.5-4.9 stars)
  - Phone number
  - Commission amount (€3)
  - "Book Now" button

**Features:**
- Search bar (filter by name or location)
- Dropdown filters: Cuisine type, Price range
- Clear call-to-action buttons

**Sample data:**
- Taverna Plaka | Greek | Plaka | €€€ | ⭐4.8
- Italian Corner | Italian | Kolonaki | €€€ | ⭐4.7
- Seafood Paradise | Seafood | Piraeus | €€€€ | ⭐4.9

### VIEW 3: EARNINGS
**Purpose:** Track commission earnings

**Content to display:**
- 3 stat cards:
  - Total Earnings (€15 example)
  - Pending Earnings (€6 example)
  - Commission Rate (€3.00 per booking)

- Breakdown table by restaurant:
  - Restaurant name
  - Completed bookings count
  - Confirmed bookings count
  - Total earned amount
  - Pending amount

**Sample data:**
- Taverna Plaka: 2 completed (€6), 1 confirmed (€3 pending)
- Italian Corner: 1 completed (€3), 0 confirmed
- Plaka Grill: 2 completed (€6), 1 confirmed (€3 pending)

---

## 3. DESIGN STYLE PREFERENCES

**Overall Feel:**
Professional, clean, trustworthy, data-focused

**Layout:**
- Dense/efficient (more info visible, less scrolling)
- Full-width content (no wasted space)
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Easy to scan

**Inspiration:**
- Booking.com (for reservation management)
- Stripe Dashboard (for data/earnings display)
- Airbnb Host Dashboard (for clean professional look)

**Color Scheme:**
- Professional blue as primary accent
- Clean whites/grays for backgrounds
- Status colors: Yellow (pending), Blue (confirmed), Green (completed)
- High contrast for readability

**Typography:**
- Clear hierarchy
- Professional sans-serif fonts
- Good readability for data-heavy tables

**Components:**
- Tables for bookings (preferred over cards)
- Cards OR list view for restaurants (designer's choice)
- Stat cards for earnings metrics
- Clean buttons with clear actions
- Status badges/pills
- Search inputs and filter dropdowns

---

## 4. TECHNICAL CONSTRAINTS

**Platform:** React web app
**Layout:** Desktop-first (responsive not required)
**Data Display:** ~5-10 items per section
**No animations required** (but welcome if subtle)

---

## 5. WHAT TO AVOID

❌ Overly colorful/playful designs
❌ Too much empty white space
❌ Cluttered layouts
❌ Confusing navigation
❌ Small text that's hard to read
❌ Hidden features in dropdowns

## SAMPLE USER FLOW

1. Concierge logs in → Sees "My Bookings" dashboard
2. Checks today's reservations → Filters by "Confirmed"
3. Guest asks for restaurant → Switches to "Browse Restaurants"
4. Searches "Plaka" → Finds "Taverna Plaka"
5. Clicks "Book Now" → Makes reservation
6. End of month → Checks "Earnings" to see commission
